DepEd Zambo welcomed us; we got recruited.

JB
James Benedict February 19, 2026
Week 1
The first week of my internship at the Department of Education – Zamboanga City Division has been an incredible start. Stepping into the ICT Unit, I was immediately immersed in a mission-critical project: developing a centralized Learning Management System (LMS) for the division to facilitate the distribution of accessible learning materials.
Collaborating with my co-intern, we focused on building the core foundation of the LMS. We successfully designed the database structure to support users and learning materials, while crafting the landing page and the main system dashboard. This week was all about establishing the "skeleton" of the system, including the crucial backend logic for user authentication (login and registration), even before we started applying styling.
Transitioning into real-world development brought its own set of hurdles. We faced a steep learning curve getting started with the Laravel framework. Additionally, we navigated some installation and PHP version compatibility issues, which required careful debugging of our configurations. These experiences were invaluable, pushing us to sharpen our problem-solving skills and learn how to manage development environments effectively.
It wasn't just about code; we also gained insight into institutional needs during coordination meetings, where we discussed the importance of automated systems in improving public service delivery. Applying concepts from my database and programming courses to this project has been incredibly rewarding. We’re currently at about 8–10% progress, and with the core foundation laid, I’m excited to dive into UI enhancements with Tailwind CSS in the coming weeks.
